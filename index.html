About

Welcome to YantraYodha, the hardware project club at Abacus Institute of Engineering and Management! Our club is dedicated to exploring the realms of electronics, programming, and robotics while nurturing a community of learners and innovators.

At YantraYodha, we believe in the power of knowledge sharing. Our senior students take on the role of mentors, guiding juniors through the intricacies of programming languages, microcontrollers, electronic sensors, IoT (Internet of Things), Linux, web development, and more. Through interactive sessions, workshops, and hands-on projects, we aim to equip our members with the skills and confidence needed to thrive in today's technology-driven world.

One of the cornerstones of YantraYodha is our commitment to fostering innovation and creativity. We organize various project competitions where students can showcase their ingenuity and problem-solving abilities. These competitions not only provide a platform for students to demonstrate their technical prowess but also encourage collaboration and teamwork.

In addition to competitions, we regularly host hands-on workshops where members can dive deep into specific technologies or concepts. Whether it's learning to build a robot from scratch or mastering the intricacies of IoT development, our workshops offer practical, real-world experience that complements classroom learning.

Furthermore, YantraYodha organizes seminars and guest lectures featuring experts from industry and academia. These events expose our members to the latest advancements and trends in technology, inspiring them to push the boundaries of what's possible.
